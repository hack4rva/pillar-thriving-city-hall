## Working Direction
- Pillar: A Thriving City Hall
- Candidate problem: Helping City Staff Review Procurement Risks and Opportunities
- Likely user: Procurement analyst or contract review staff member in a Richmond department
- Why it matters: Staff currently spend too much time manually scanning contracts to find expiration dates, renewal terms, and key risk signals before making a recommendation.
- Why it seems weekend-buildable: We can combine the City contracts dataset with the sample procurement PDFs in `procurement-examples/` and deliver a focused dashboard + contract detail workflow.
- Biggest uncertainty: OCR quality and inconsistent contract formatting may reduce extraction accuracy for dates, amounts, and renewal language unless manually verified in the PDF.
